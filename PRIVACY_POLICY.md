## 2. Who This App Is For
This app is intended for children and families, including children under 13, and supports:

- Ages 6–8 with parent/guardian involvement
- Ages 8+ for independent educational play

We aim to comply with:

- U.S. Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
- Google Play Families Policy

## 3. Information We Collect
We do **not** collect personal information directly from users in the app, including:

- Name, email, phone number, home address
- Contacts, photos, microphone recordings
- Precise location
- Biometric data
- User-generated public profile data

No account or login is required.

## 4. Local Device Data
The app stores gameplay data locally on the device, such as:

- Progress
- Scores/achievements
- Settings/preferences

This local data is not transmitted to our servers.

You can delete local data by:

- Uninstalling the app, or
- Clearing app storage in device settings

## 5. Payments and Google Play
Benny’s Mathanomical Adventure is a paid app distributed via Google Play. Payment processing is handled by Google Play under Google’s own terms and privacy policy. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

## 6. Ads, Analytics, and Third Parties
- No third-party advertising in the app
- No behavioral advertising
- No third-party analytics tracking SDKs (if true)
- No sale of personal information

If we add any third-party data practices in the future, we will update this policy before those changes apply.

## 7. Permissions

## 8. Children’s Privacy (COPPA)
